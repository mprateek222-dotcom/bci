About BCI

Empowering Industries, Promoting Trade & Driving Growth Since 2013

Who We Are

Established in 2013, Bawana Chamber of Industries (BCI) is one of the leading industrial associations representing industries and businesses operating in Bawana Industrial Area, Delhi.

The Chamber works continuously to create and sustain an environment conducive to industrial growth, entrepreneurship, trade promotion and economic development. BCI acts as a bridge between industries and government authorities, ensuring that the concerns and interests of industrial units are effectively represented.

Our Role

Bawana Chamber of Industries serves as a catalyst for industrial development by promoting policy advocacy, industrial networking, compliance awareness, training initiatives and member support services.

The Chamber provides guidance related to re-registration services, NOC facilitation, industrial compliance support, government liaison activities and business development opportunities.

Our Vision

To create a progressive industrial ecosystem that promotes innovation, sustainable growth, entrepreneurship and economic prosperity for industries in Bawana and across India.

Our Mission

  • Promote industrial growth and development.
  • Represent member industries effectively.
  • Strengthen industry-government collaboration.
  • Provide support and guidance to industrial units.
  • Organize training programs and workshops.
  • Enhance competitiveness of member industries.

Training & Development

BCI regularly organizes Human Resource Development Programs, Industrial Workshops, Business Seminars, Networking Events and Industry Discussions to improve management practices, professional skills and operational efficiency.

These initiatives help member industries stay updated with evolving regulations, market trends and emerging business opportunities.
