About BCI

Empowering Industries, Promoting Trade & Driving Growth Since 2013

Who We Are

Bawana Chamber of Industries (BCI), established in 2013, is one of the leading industrial associations dedicated to representing and supporting industries operating in the Bawana Industrial Area, Delhi.

Over the years, BCI has emerged as a trusted platform for industrialists, entrepreneurs, manufacturers and business owners by providing guidance, advocacy, networking opportunities and industry support services.

The Chamber works closely with government authorities, regulatory bodies and stakeholders to create a business-friendly environment that promotes sustainable industrial growth and economic development.

Our Vision

To create a progressive, innovative and globally competitive industrial ecosystem that encourages entrepreneurship, supports sustainable development and contributes to the economic growth of the nation.

Our Mission

Represent and protect the interests of member industries.
Promote industrial growth, innovation and business excellence.
Facilitate communication between industries and government authorities.
Provide professional support and industrial guidance.
Organize seminars, workshops and training programs.
Strengthen collaboration within the industrial community.

Why Choose BCI?

BCI serves as a catalyst for industrial development through industry representation, policy advocacy, government liaison support, re-registration assistance, NOC facilitation, industrial compliance guidance and business networking initiatives.

Our members benefit from professional guidance, valuable networking opportunities, knowledge-sharing platforms and regular industry updates that help businesses remain competitive and future-ready.

Our Motto

"Hamesha Ek Kadam Aage"

Committed to supporting industries, promoting growth and building a stronger industrial community.
