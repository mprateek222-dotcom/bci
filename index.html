About BCI

Established in 2013, Bawana Chamber of Industries (BCI) is a leading industrial association representing industries and businesses operating in Bawana Industrial Area, Delhi.


BCI acts as a catalyst in the promotion of industry, trade and entrepreneurship through policy advocacy, industrial representation, networking opportunities, seminars and member support services.
