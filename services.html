Our Services

Supporting Industries Through Professional Guidance, Representation & Development

Services We Offer

Member Industry Support

Providing dedicated assistance and guidance to industries operating in Bawana Industrial Area. We help members address operational challenges and stay informed about industry developments.

Re-registration Assistance

Professional support for industrial registrations, renewals, documentation and procedural requirements to ensure smooth business operations.

NOC Facilitation

Assistance in obtaining No Objection Certificates and various approvals required for industrial and business activities.

Government Liaison Services

Acting as a bridge between industries and government departments to resolve issues and represent the interests of member industries.

Training & Development Programs

Organizing workshops, seminars, leadership sessions and skill development programs to enhance business performance and productivity.

Business Networking

Creating opportunities for collaboration, partnerships and growth through networking events, industry meetings and business interactions.

Why Choose BCI?


Bawana Chamber of Industries is committed to strengthening the industrial ecosystem through advocacy, professional support, knowledge-sharing and industry representation. Our mission is to help businesses grow, remain competitive and contribute to economic development.
